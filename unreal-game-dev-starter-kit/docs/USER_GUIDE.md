## How to “apply” the kit

1. **Pick one habit** per week (e.g. consistent `Content/` top-level folders) instead of rewriting your whole project overnight.  
2. **Track decisions** in your own `DECISIONS.md` or team wiki—this kit does not replace your design docs.  
3. **Adapt** naming and folder examples to your genre (FPS vs. RTS vs. narrative) without throwing away the principles (ownership, reviewability, binary vs. mergeable files).

## What this kit does *not* do

- It does not install plugins, assets, or code into your project.  
- It does not replace Epic’s official documentation, samples, or learning courses.  
- It does not guarantee shipping dates—only fewer self-inflicted workflow failures.
